Classic Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g guanciale or pancetta
  • 2 large eggs + 1 yolk
  • 50g Pecorino Romano, grated
  • Black pepper, salt

Method

  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Render guanciale in a cold pan over medium heat.
  3. Whisk eggs with cheese and pepper.
  4. Combine off heat, tossing with pasta water to emulsify.
