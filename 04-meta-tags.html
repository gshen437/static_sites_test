City Council Approves New Riverside Park

In a 5-2 vote Tuesday evening, the city council approved the development of a new 12-acre park along the eastern riverbank. Construction is expected to begin in spring and complete within 18 months. The park will include walking trails, a playground, and a small amphitheater.

Councilmember Rivera, who voted in favor, called it "a long-overdue investment in green space for the community."
